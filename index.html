RAMPART FIREARMS

Guns, Ammo, Hunting, Tactical and Personal Defense


Rampart Firearms is open and offering firearms, ammunition, holsters, cleaning, safety gear, tactical supplies and support. Come on into our retail store nestled in a small town of Sedalia just minutes from Littleton / Castle Rock.

Our service and attention to you are what you would expect of a professional gun shop. Rampart Firearms caters to those requiring quality firearms for hunting, shooting, home and personal defense and tactical/target solutions.

We try to tailor our product line to meet the needs of our customers. If there is something you want, just let us know and we will be happy to help.

Trade or purchase of your firearm will always be considered.

Tactical / Law Enforcement

At Rampart Firearms, we have a long-standing and close relationship with law enforcement. Many of our employees work closely with local, state, and national agencies. If you have a need for tactical gear, from firearms to a new item for your duty belt, come in and see us.

Hunting

Located in Douglas County at the foot of the Rockys, Rampart Firearms is a great stop for hunting needs in any season. Shotgun, rifle, or pistol; from squirrels to your next big game trip--we can have you ready for anything.

And if you don't see what you're looking for, let us know.

Personale Defense

At Rampart Firearms, we support your decision to take responsibility for your personal safety. If you're a first-time buyer, we can help you find the gun that's right for you. And if you're an old hand, we've got everything you need to make sure your home and family are protected.

Cowboy

Cowboy action shooting is one of the fastest growing shooting sports. Naturally, we have a great selection of pistols, rifles, and shotguns to help you in your next Wild West encounter.

And if you don't see what you're looking for, let us know. We can set you up with whatever you need.

Consignments

Consignments will be taken on a 10% - 15% commission basis.

Accessories

Rampart Firearms isn't just about guns. It's about everything you need to keep your guns and yourself in proper working order for shooting sports or ready for duty.

We have range gear, targets, cleaning supplies, holsters, cases, scopes, spare mags, and on and on. Stop in and see for yourself.
