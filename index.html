Rampart Firearm


Rampart Firearms is open and offering firearms, ammunition, holsters, cleaning, safety gear, tactical supplies and support. Come on into our retail store nestled in a small town of Sedalia just minutes from Littleton / Castle Rock.

Our service and attention to you are what you would expect of a professional gun shop. Rampart Firearms caters to those requiring quality firearms for hunting, shooting, home and personal defense and tactical/target solutions.

We try to tailor our product line to meet the needs of our customers. If there is something you want, just let us know and we will be happy to help.

Trade or purchase of your firearm will always be considered.
